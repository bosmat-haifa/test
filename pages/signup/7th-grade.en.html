Registration process for the 7th grade

On Sunday (29/3/20), the registration for 7th grade will be open on the municipality site and will last till Friday (3/4/20).

The criteria for meeting the admission requirements are the grades of 6th class's first semester:
Mathematics - 85 and above.
English - 85 and above.

Please send a picture of the first semester's grade report till the end of this weekend (Thursday 26/3/20)
to this e-mail:
inna.kislev@gmail.com

We will send you an e-mail confirming compliance with the criteria so you could sign up on Sunday at the municipality.

Best of luck!
